MARS Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 28 September 2025

1. Information We Collect

MARS collects the following types of information to provide core app functionality:

  • Match and Player Data: Match scores, statistics, and player-related information entered voluntarily by users.
  • Media Content: Team photos, team logos, and player images uploaded by users.
  • App Usage Data: Non-personal analytics and performance data used to improve app stability and user experience.

2. Advertising

MARS displays advertisements to keep the app free for users.

We use Google AdMob and may work with other advertising partners. These services may collect limited information such as:

  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Approximate location based on network information
  • Ad interaction and usage data

This data is used to deliver, measure, and improve personalized or non-personalized ads.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Information

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Display and manage match and player records
  • Enable media uploads and viewing
  • Improve app performance and reliability

We do not sell user data.

4. Data Storage and Security

Information uploaded to the app is stored securely using industry-standard practices. While we take reasonable measures to protect data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for ensuring they have the rights to upload any images, logos, or media. Users should avoid uploading sensitive or confidential personal information.

6. Third-Party Services

MARS may use third-party services such as cloud storage or analytics tools. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

MARS is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page and within the app where applicable.
